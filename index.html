My Motivation Factors Towards Online Education

Online learning is not the next big thing, it is the now big thing by Donna J. Abernathy


Flexible schedule and environment

I can set up my own time to truly study at place that I am comfortable which is my bedroom.

Save cost

This is definitely true because I did not need to print out materials as I just submit it through
online medium such as google classroom, email and etc.

I can review course material repeatedly

For example, I can rewind the video content as much as I want until I truly understand it.

Deadline Develop self-discipline

Without the commitment to attend physical class, I need to manage my time and tasks together.

UiTM Institute of Continuing Education and Professional Studies chief executive officer Professor Dr Azizul Halim Yahya, who is leading the shift to ODL, said all academic activities conducted are a blend of asynchronous (without real-time interaction) and synchronous (real-time interaction) online learning.
